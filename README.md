### 🧪 About Me
I am a **Machine Learning Engineer** specialized in **NLP and Large Language Models**. My journey took me from graduating at *Università degli Studi di Roma "Tor Vergata"* to the US, where I served as a PhD Teaching Assistant at *Michigan Technological University* 🐺. 

Nowadays, I bridge the gap between rigorous data science and creative expression through **Generative Art**.

- 🔭 **Currently focusing on:** Advanced RAG architectures and LLM fine-tuning.
- 🎨 **Passionate about:** Intersection of code and aesthetics (Generative Art).
- 🏋️‍♂️ **Outside of work:** I'm into street-lifting, board games, and linguistics.
- 🇯🇵 **Learning:** Japanese (日本語を勉強しています). *継続は力なり (Perseverance is power).*
